Lopez Origins

Born in Bogotá, I nurtured a fascination and admiration for technology and globalization. With the rise of social media and mobile devices, I realized what I wanted to be a part of, in this world full of volatility, uncertainty, complexity and ambiguity.

Knowledge Trek

Enrolled in an International Baccalaureate Organization-affiliated school, I was exposed to various concepts and methodologies that significantly broadened my perspective on life. During my time at this institution, I seized the chance to attend a summer camp at Stanford University in San Francisco, where my fascination with technology began to blossom.

Beyond Graduation

During my high school graduation process, I experienced one of the strangest events in recent history: the COVID-19 pandemic. As a result, I was unable to fully enjoy my final school years and graduation. However, I continued my education through self-study and informal learning, delving into diverse subjects such as development engineering, digital content, personal branding, and digital marketing.

Exit, Enter Life

Projects

Smart Home IoT Project

This project is part of my coursework for the Internet of Things class at DeVry University. It simulates a smart home automation and security system using virtual tools. The system includes a door sensor, distance sensor, and automatic lighting to monitor and control home conditions. It demonstrates core IoT concepts such as sensor integration, automation logic, and virtual prototyping.
